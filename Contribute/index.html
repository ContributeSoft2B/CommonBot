Principles and Design for GymChain

GymChain is dedicated to help everyone build a reasonable and funny sport and fitness scene. We use AI smart wearable device to collect users’ sport and health information, and upload it to the chain. Users will get feed back afterwards through big data analysis with scientific exercise methods guide and practical exercise plan. GymChain makes it possible for everyone to participate in sports and fitness to obtain better experience and share the commercial value of taking part of the GymChain exercise. For this purpose, GymChain is designed based on the following logic and principles:

Reasonable

Every natural person and other business forms can participate in this ecosystem. We ensure that all parties benefit from our reasonable design so that GymChain ecosystem gets a stable and long-term development.

Open & Share

GymChain platform is open to every fitness and health related individual and institution. Every party is welcomed to share its skills, information, and equipment so that the GymChain value and benefits for all participants get increased.

Fairness

The system treats each participant fairly, using decentralized block chain voting mechanism and intelligent contracts, a well acknowledged system by all.

Transparent

All transactions, exchanges, mining, and social rules are built and executed on the basis of block chains and Smart Contract. We guarantee that every pay and resource will receive reasonable return and that the whole transaction process is open, transparent, and cannot be altered.

Free

In the system, each participant can freely exchange skills, produce transactions, social and mining jointly.

Interesting

We bring in interesting features for everyone to do sports, take exercises, social and make money in a relaxing and enjoyable environment.

GymChain application scenes

GCC application scene

Universal mining

Users can start fitness mode at any time of the day. When intelligent hardware detects the user's sporting speed, accelerated operation mode will automatically start. Users will be given a certain amount of token based on their exercising situation according to the scoring scheme. This will also encourage users to develop the habit of continuous exercising.

Clients diversion

Fitness venues connects to the GymChain system and reward clients with GCC purchased the via third-party platform, in order to attract more clients to join the venues.

Increasing value of GCC

The amount of the GCC remaining constant, the value of the GCC will increase with the speed of flow. As the GCC revalues, we will continue to develop more scenarios for GCC trading. In theory, any commercial activity can be connected to GCC and there would be many scenarios for its application.

Consumption in fitness venues

Users can pay their consumption with received GCC in fitness venues connected to the GymChain system.

Purchase game props

Users can purchase services offered by fitness venues with GCC, and fitness venues can attract clients by providing GCC.

Value delivery

Users get a certain amount of token reward for their fitness exercise. Tokens can be used to support consumption in fitness centers. Users’ efforts in exercises are thus transformed into value.
